Pioneering Mars Exploration

GUB Mars Rover Team is a multidisciplinary student organization dedicated to advancing space technology and preparing the next generation of engineers and scientists for interplanetary exploration.

Our Technical Divisions

Electrical Systems

Power Management

Designing robust power distribution systems and custom battery solutions to ensure uninterrupted operation in extreme conditions.

Embedded Hardware

Developing custom PCBs for sensor integration and motor control with fail-safe mechanisms for critical systems.

Communications

Implementing reliable RF systems with redundant communication channels for mission-critical data transmission.

Software Systems

Autonomous Navigation

Developing advanced pathfinding algorithms using sensor fusion from GPS, IMUs, and computer vision systems.

Perception Systems

Implementing real-time object detection and terrain analysis using stereo vision and LIDAR data processing.

Teleoperation

Creating intuitive control interfaces with haptic feedback and system health monitoring dashboards.

Mechanical Systems

Robotic Manipulation

Designing 6-DOF arms with force feedback and precision end-effectors for complex servicing tasks.

Sample Handling

Developing contamination-free sample acquisition and preparation systems for in-situ analysis.

Mobility Systems

Engineering rocker-bogie suspension systems optimized for Martian terrain traversal.

Scientific Research

Astrobiology

Developing life detection protocols and miniature lab-on-rover systems for field analysis.

GUB Mars Rover Team is a student organization dedicated to advancing space robotics technology through innovation and competition.
